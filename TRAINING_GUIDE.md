### Getting Started

#### Demo Credentials

Use these accounts to explore the system:

- **Admin**: `admin@example.com` / `admin123`
- **Employee**: `employee@example.com` / `emp123`

#### 1. Logging In

1. Navigate to your CRM URL (e.g., `https://your-crm.com`)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

#### 2. Dashboard Overview

After login, you'll see:

- **Customer Count** - Total registered customers
- **Lead Count** - Active leads being followed
- **Open Tickets** - Support tickets awaiting response

---

### Managing Customers

#### Add a Customer

1. Click **Customers** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Customer** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - Name (required)
   - Phone
   - Email
   - Company
   - Address
4. Click **Create Customer**

#### View Customer Details

1. Click on any customer row
2. View contact information, interaction history, and tickets

---

### Managing Leads

#### Add a Lead

1. Click **Leads** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Lead**
3. Fill in contact details
4. Click **Create Lead**

#### Change Lead Status

1. Open lead details
2. Use the status dropdown:
   - **New** - Just added
   - **Under Follow-up** - Active communication
   - **Interested** - Showing interest
   - **Converted** - Became a customer

#### Convert Lead to Customer

1. Open lead details
2. Click **Convert to Customer**
3. Confirm the action
4. Lead becomes a customer automatically

---

### Logging Activities

   - ๐ง Email
   - ๐ Call

### Managing Tickets

#### Create a Ticket

1. Open a Customer details page
2. Click **Add Ticket**
3. Enter:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Priority (Low/Medium/High)
4. Click **Create Ticket**

#### Update Ticket Status

1. Open ticket details
2. Change status:
   - **Open** - New ticket
   - **In Progress** - Being worked on
   - **Closed** - Resolved

#### Add Message to Ticket

1. Open ticket details
2. Type message in the text area
3. Click **Send**

---

### Reports (Admin Only)

#### View Reports

1. Click **Reports** in sidebar
2. View metrics:
   - Total Customers
   - Total Leads
   - Conversion Rate
   - Employee Performance table

#### Export Data

1. Click **Export Excel** to download CSV
2. Use for analysis in Excel/Google Sheets

---

### User Management (Admin Only)

#### Add a User

1. Click **User Management** in sidebar
2. Click **Add User**
3. Enter:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Password
   - Role (Admin/Employee)
4. Click **Save**

#### Edit/Delete Users

- Click pencil icon to edit
- Click trash icon to delete (confirmation required)

---

### Global Search

1. Use the search bar at the top of the sidebar
2. Type any name, email, or keyword
3. Press Enter
4. View results organized by category

---

### Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the sidebar
2. View unread notifications (badge shows count)
3. Click a notification to mark as read
4. Click **Mark all as read** to clear all

---
